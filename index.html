About Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is an American animated franchise and series of comedy short films created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera.
Best known for its 161 theatrical short films by Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer, the series centers on a friendship/rivalry between the title characters Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse.

Tom is always trying to catch Jerry

Let's meet few of the cartoon's characters!

Click on links below to learn more about them.

  • Tom is a blue (sometimes blue) and white domestic shorthair cat.
  • Jerry is a small, brown, house mouse who always lives in close proximity to Tom.
  • Spike is a stern but occasionally dumb grey American bulldog who is particularly disapproving of cats, but is gentle towards mice
  • Butch is the leader of the alley cat bullies who are usually friends with Tom and help him catch Jerry.
  • Toodles Galore is an attractive white female cat, who usually wears a large blue bow around her neck, and is supposedly Tom's girlfriend, although Tom is a reputed playboy.
  • Mammy Two Shoes is an African-American woman: whether or not she is a maid taking care of the home in which Tom and Jerry reside or the homeowner is never made clear.
